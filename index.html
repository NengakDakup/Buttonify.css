How to use

Buttonify.css is a simple CSS Library with lots of helper classes to help you style your buttons easily. You can even add pseudo-effects to your buttons by simply adding classes...

To use it in your webpages, you have to download the buttonify.css or buttonify.min.css (the compressed version) and include it in your webpages

Sizes

You can specify the sizes of the buttons by adding the classes .sm for small sized buttons, .md for medium sized buttons, .lg for large sized buttons

will provide the following result:

the default size when no size is specified is the medium size .bty-md

Colors

You can specify the colors for the buttons. There are 6 colors which can be specified for the buttons, .primary gives a light blue color, .info gives this greenish-blue color, .danger gives red color, .success gives a green color, and .medium gives an orange color, .trans gives a button with transparent background

Shapes

You can also specify the shapes of the buttons which are as follows: .circle which gives a perfectly rounded button, .rect give a rectangle shaped button, .norm gives the normal default button with an average border-radius, and .curved gives a button with greater border-radius

Dropdowns

adding the following classes will provide different types of dropdowns accordingly: .dropclick will provide a button that will produce a dropdown when clicked, .drophover will provide a button that will display a dropdown when hovered
