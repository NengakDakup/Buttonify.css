Overview

Buttonify.css is a simple CSS Library with lots of helper classes to help you style your buttons easily. You can even add pseudo-effects to your buttons by simply adding classes...

To use it in your webpages, you have to download the buttonify.css or buttonify.min.css (the compressed version) and include it in your webpages

Sizes

You can specify the sizes of the buttons by adding the classes .sm for small sized buttons, .md for medium sized buttons, .lg for large sized buttons

Colors

You can specify the colors for the buttons. There are 5 colors which can be specified for the buttons, primary gives a light blue color, info gives this greenish-blue color, danger gives red color, success gives a green color, and medium gives an orange color
